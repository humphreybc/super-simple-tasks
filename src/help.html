Clear completed tasks

Click Clear completed to remove all completed tasks.

Share your list with someone

  1. Click Sharing in the top right.
  2. Click Share this list with someone.
  3. Send the link to your friend. When they open the link, your lists will be connected.

Sync tasks to your Android device

  1. Install Super Simple Tasks for Android.
  2. Click Sharing in the top right.
  3. Click Share this list with someone.
  4. Open Super Simple Tasks on your Android phone or tablet.
  5. Tap Sharing in the top right.
  6. Tap Join an existing list.
  7. Enter the code from step 2 and tap the green button.

Sync tasks to your iOS device

  1. Click Sharing in the top right.
  2. Click Share this list with someone.
  3. On your iOS device, open supersimpletasks.com.
  4. Tap Sharing in the top right.
  5. Tap Join an existing list.
  6. Enter the code from step 2 and tap the green button.

Tip: If you’re using Chrome, tap Menu and choose Add to Home screen to put Super Simple Tasks on your home screen.

Connect multiple Chrome extensions

  1. Click Sharing in the top right.
  2. Click Share this list with someone.
  3. On your other computer, open the extension and click Sharing.
  4. Click Join an existing list.
  5. Enter the code from step 2 and click the green button.

Disconnect from a shared list

Click Sharing in the top right and click Disconnect this list. This will stop Super Simple Tasks from connecting to the remote server, and only use tasks stored locally.

Print tasks

To print your tasks hit CMD + P (OS X) or CTRL + P (Windows) on your keyboard.
